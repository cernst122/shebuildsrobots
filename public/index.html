LET'S BUILD ROBOTS

Circuit & Coding Projects For Girls

The world of robotics is exciting, creative, and fun. But by high school, too many girls have heard the message that a tech career isn't for them. She Builds Robots is a collection of introductory circuit and coding projects designed for girls in middle school and above, written by a Google engineer and seeded by the 1517 Fund.

Build circuit confidence with nine beginner-friendly projects.

STEM education doesn't have to mean lab work. When e-textile circuits were introduced in a study of middle school classrooms, girls tended to play a greater leadership role than boys, and the projects were positioned in front of girls 81% of the time1. Explore e-textile projects from wall art to color-changing dresses.
